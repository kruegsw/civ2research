1.  Introduction.

The almighty Hidden Foundation lurks somewhere. The United Nations struggle to free the World from 
the Hidden menace.

2.  Races.

Hidden is a world inhabited by seven civilizations:

	 * The Hidden Foundation: This Civ is located in an island. Three invisible cities guarded by 
invisible units are waiting their revenge. Hidden knowledge is vast, including Invisibility, which allows 
the invisible units Aerobics (flying) and Invisible Man. The well-defended island is under UN blockade.

	 * United Nations: With their Blue Helmets units try to stop the fury of  the Hidden 
Foundation. They are allied with the G-Philes.

	* G-Philes: Their FBI Agents search for the unknown. They have detected UFO activity 
somewhere and want to hunt some alien being, preferably alive and kicking.

	* Martians: Coming from Mars, their only mission is exploring and destruction of  any 
human. They were guided by a radio emission from the Hidden Foundation. Their UFO unit is extremely 
powerful. Fortunately, the Martians units are so far in a lonely island.

	* Freaks: A race of monsters, they are in the Stone Age. The Monster unit is a mutated 
Elephant.

	* Paranoids: Their exteme Paranoia, a unique tech that they have discovered, caused them an 
altered state of mind. The Paranoid units don’t stop to ask.

	* Revolutionaries: A fighting race, wanting to lead the rest of the world under their ideology. 
Their Guerrilleros might be strong enough to accomplish that task.
