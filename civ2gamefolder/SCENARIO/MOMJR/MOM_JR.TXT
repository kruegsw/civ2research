^Arcanus is a land similar in many ways to medieval earth.  The primary exception of course,
^being the power of magic and the presence of many fantastic creatures created with it.  
^Magic itself is composed of 5 primary forces: Life - representing the positive forces of 
^protection and purity, Nature - composed of all natural forces and the element of Earth, 
^Sorcery - the power of the mind and the elements of Air and Water, Chaos - represents 
^destruction and the element of Fire and finally Death - the negative power of the Undead 
^and decay.
^Into this world you have emerged as the champion of one of these elemental forces of magic.
^You will start the game with a wizard unit representing yourself and a small band of 
^peasants.  Your mission is to create a mighty empire from these humble beginnings and prove
^the superiority of your magical abilities.
^
^PLEASE NOTE
^Only complete victory will suffice to win this scenario.  All other tribes must be
^completely eliminated. Any of the five tribes will provide a challeging game.  Each has
