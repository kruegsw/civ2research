4. **FUN_0040ac5a (`generate_rivers`)** - River generation algorithm with random-walk carving, terrain mutation, and backtracking. Reveals how rivers are placed and why they raise terrain types.

5. **FUN_00406e61 (`minimap_get_tile_color`)** - Minimap color logic showing visibility, ownership, goto-path rendering. Shows how fog of war interacts with the minimap display.
